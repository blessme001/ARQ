About Us

Global Expertise in Commodity Trading & Agricultural Consulting

Our Legacy

ARA GREENFIELD LIMITED is a premier global merchant specializing in commodity exports, trading, and agricultural consulting with an unparalleled global presence. Our expertise encompasses a sophisticated portfolio of commodities including rice, corn, sesame, barley, sorghum, oats, cocoa, beans, wheat, soybeans, and precious metals.

We ensure a reliable supply of premium-quality products for both domestic and international buyers through our accomplished leadership team, proprietary market intelligence tools, and extensive supplier network.

Dedicated to advancing the future of global commodity trade while championing sustainable agriculture across emerging markets.

Company Overview

Strategic Excellence in Global Trade

Strategic Excellence

ARA GREENFIELD LIMITED stands as a strategic grain exporter and agricultural consulting firm, powered by the exceptional expertise of Crippen International LLC—our esteemed partner with over three decades of specialized experience in grain and seed processing systems and Impact Grains Limited.

Leveraging Crippen International's unrivaled technical expertise, earned through operations spanning over 80 countries, we deliver comprehensive solutions tailored for farmers, millers, and industrial buyers alike.

Our integrated strengths ensure efficient grain distribution, innovative processing insights, and globally competitive pricing—all underscored by decades of excellence in post-harvest handling and bulk storage solutions.
